⚠️ Legal Basis for This Form Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 — Section 9  ·  DPDP Rules, 2025  ·  IT Act, 2000  ·  IT (SPDI) Rules, 2011
Under the DPDP Act, 2023, personal data of any individual below 18 years of age may only be processed after obtaining verifiable consent from a parent or lawful guardian. This form records that consent.

3 Consent Declarations

Please read each item carefully. Tick the checkbox next to each point to confirm your understanding and consent. All items are mandatory. This form is not valid unless all boxes are checked.
  • Identity & Authority: I confirm that I am the parent / lawful guardian of the student named in Section 1, and I am legally authorised to provide consent on their behalf under Indian law.
  • Age Confirmation: I confirm that the student is below 18 years of age at the time of this registration, and therefore this parental consent is required before any personal data is processed.
  • Data Collection — Student: I consent to StudentsAttendanceApp collecting and storing the following data about my child: full name, roll number, class, section, date-wise attendance records (Present / Absent / Late), and the email address used for login.
  • Data Collection — Parent Contact: I consent to my mobile phone number being stored in the App as a parent contact linked to my child's attendance record. This number may be used by the school to communicate attendance-related information to me.
  • Purpose of Processing: I understand that my child's data will be used exclusively for school attendance management — to mark, view, and report attendance. It will not be used for advertising, profiling, AI/ML training, or any commercial purpose.
  • Data Storage & Security: I understand that data is stored on Supabase (a secure cloud database provider) and is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Access to my child's data is restricted to authorised Teachers and the School Principal only.
  • No Sharing with Third Parties: I understand that my child's personal data will not be sold, rented, or shared with any third party for commercial purposes. It is shared with Razorpay (payment processing) and Supabase/Render.com (infrastructure) only as required to operate the Service, under contractual data protection obligations.
  • Cross-Border Transfer: I understand that data may be stored on servers located outside India (USA/EU) via Supabase's infrastructure, under appropriate safeguards, and I consent to this transfer.
  • Data Retention: I understand that my child's data will be retained for the duration of the school's active subscription and deleted within 90 days of account closure, unless a longer retention period is required by Indian law.
  • My Rights (DPDP Act, 2023): I understand that I have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of my child's personal data at any time by contacting the Grievance Officer at studentsattendanceapp@gmail.com. Requests will be processed within 30 days.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: I understand that I may withdraw this consent at any time by submitting a written request to the school or to studentsattendanceapp@gmail.com. Withdrawal will result in my child's account and data being removed within 30 days. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing done before withdrawal.
  • Terms & Privacy Policy: I confirm that I have read and agree to the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy of StudentsAttendanceApp (available at the school office or online), and I agree that these documents govern the use of the App on behalf of my child.
