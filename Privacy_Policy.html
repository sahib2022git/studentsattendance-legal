1Introduction & Who We Are

StudentsAttendanceApp ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of all users — Principals, Teachers, Students, and Parents. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable Indian law.

Role clarification under DPDP Act, 2023:
The School (Principal) is the primary Data Fiduciary — they decide what student data to collect and upload.
StudentsAttendanceApp acts as a Data Processor — we process data only on the School's instructions and for no other purpose.

3Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contractual Necessity — Account and attendance data are essential to deliver the Service under your subscription agreement.
  • Legitimate Interest — Diagnostic/technical data for app stability.
  • School's Authority — Student and parent data is uploaded by the School under their institutional authority and existing parent-school relationship. The School is responsible for obtaining any necessary parental consent.
Schools' Responsibility: By uploading student and parent data, the School confirms it has a lawful basis to share this data with us (e.g., parent consent via admission forms, institutional policy, or applicable statutory obligation). We rely on this representation.

4Children's Data (Under 18)

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, students below 18 years are classified as children and receive heightened protection.

  • Student accounts are never self-created by students. They are created by the School's Principal or Clerk or Parents on behalf of the student.
  • We do not use student data for profiling, behavioural analytics, or targeted advertising.
  • We do not allow children to independently register, access data of other students, or modify their own records.
  • Student data is used exclusively for attendance management within the student's own school.
  • The School (as Data Fiduciary) must ensure verifiable parental/guardian consent is obtained before uploading a child's data to our platform, as required by the DPDP Act and DPDP Rules, 2025.

5How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the attendance management service.
  • To manage user accounts and authenticate logins.
  • To process subscription payments and issue receipts.
  • To send transactional emails (OTP for password reset, account notifications) — no marketing emails without consent.
  • To generate attendance reports for Teachers and Principals.
  • To diagnose technical issues and maintain app stability.
  • To comply with applicable Indian laws and respond to lawful government requests.
We do NOT use your data for: Targeted advertising, selling to third parties, training AI/ML models, cross-school profiling, or any purpose beyond operating this Service.

7Data Security

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — authentication is handled by Supabase Auth (industry-standard bcrypt hashing).
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Data at rest is encrypted on Supabase's infrastructure.
  • API keys and secrets are stored server-side only and never baked into the mobile app.
  • Login is protected by a 5-attempt lockout mechanism to prevent brute-force attacks.
  • OTP-based password reset with 10-minute expiry for account recovery.
  • Access to student data is role-based: Teachers can only view data for their own assigned classes; Students can only view their own records.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected Schools within a reasonable time as required by law.

9Your Rights as a Data Principal (DPDP Act, 2023)

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the following rights (enforceable from May 2027, but honoured by us now):

  • Right to Access — Request a summary of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Grievance Redressal — Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer.
  • Right to Nominate — Nominate a person to exercise your rights in case of incapacity or death.

To exercise any right, contact our Grievance Officer (Section 13 below). We will respond within 30 days.

For school-managed student accounts, requests should be made by the School Principal on behalf of the student/parent.

10Cookies & Tracking

The mobile App does not use cookies. The Clerk Portal (web-based) uses session-only functionality limited to managing the active upload session — no tracking or advertising cookies are used.

11Cross-Border Data Transfer

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure which may be hosted in the USA or EU. Such transfers are governed by Supabase's data processing agreements and are conducted with appropriate safeguards. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

12Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal changes or improvements to our practices. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email to the Principal at least 15 days before taking effect.
